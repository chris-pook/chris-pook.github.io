Magento2 Migration

We configured the core Magento 2 features to provide many improvements over their proprietary system.

The perfect combination of Varnish and Redis Cache gave huge performance boosts in page loading speeds.

PC Configurator products could be easily created via the Magento Admin Panel, making complex combinations simple.

A lightning fast Magento 2 website with a custom PC Configurator.

Custom Magento 2 Product Type

Bespoke PC Configurator product with infinite component combinations.

Magento 2 comes with various product types out of the box including Configurable Product and Bundle Product, however DinoPC needed something a little more Dinosauric!

The main requirement of their new website was to be able to display hundreds of components to a customer and allow them to pick and choose freely to build their dream PC.

Absolute Commerce created a new product type, based loosely on the Magento Bundle Product, but allowing for infinite combinations of component products.

Component Validation

Not all computer components are compatible together, so DinoPC needed to be able to validate the combinations based on business logic.

We developed a complex filtering and validation system which the team could manage via the Magento Admin Panel, allowing complete control over what the customer could see and select, and provide the customer with instant feedback on invalid combinations.

PC Configurator validation provides instant feedback on selections.
