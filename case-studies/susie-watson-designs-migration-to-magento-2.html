Susie Watson Designs migrate to Magento 2

At Susie Watson Designs, all of the products have been handmade with dedication and care by a team of highly skilled individuals. They brought us in to bring this same level of detail to the construction of their new Magento ecommerce platform.

What We Did

Susie had decided Absolute Commerce was the perfect partner to migrate her website to Magento 2. A small team sharing the same values, offering the same care and attention to quality, delivered by highly skilled individuals who take great pride in their work.

Susie Watson Designs already had an ecommerce website. Their existing Magento 1 store was functional, but slow and stagnant. Due to many years of development by different parties, making any changes would usually involve a high risk of regression issues.

Some of the biggest miracles Chris and Joseph performed took place behind the scenes.

I can't believe how smoothly the site launch went, and by getting it up and running in time for the launch of our Christmas catalogue, Absolute Commerce achieved the impossible.

Sales have been through the roof, all thanks to the work done by Absolute Commerce.

Fraser Mackenzie Web Manager, Susie Watson Designs

In order to focus on more exciting new features, first they needed a solid framework with high integrity data and a stable integration with their ERP. Having discussed the various options available with Absolute Commerce, they decided a migration to Magento 2 was the optimal solution.

Some project highlights include:

  • A full Magento 2 rebuild from scratch.
  • Data Migration from Magento 1 to Magento 2.
  • A lightning fast store with CDN, Varnish and Redis Cache.
  • Powerful Elasticsearch integration of Product, Category and CMS Content.
  • Fully responsive, mobile-first theme.
  • Seamless integration with Netsuite ERP via Celigo.
  • Gift Card purchase, balance and redemption functionality.
  • Project discovery, management, development and deployment.

Magento2 Migration

We configured the core Magento 2 features to provide many much needed improvements.

The perfect combination of CloudFront CDN, Varnish and Redis Cache gives huge performance boosts in page loading speeds.

Via the Admin Panel, the Susie Watson Designs team can manage all content across the customised responsive, mobile-first theme with streamlined checkout.

A lightning fast, fully responsive, mobile-first Magento 2 website.

Bespoke extension development

A custom Product Detail Page showcases the beauty of the Susie Watson Designs pottery.

On top of this strong framework, we created bespoke extensions especially for Susie Watson Designs in order to fulfil their requirements for showcasing their catalogue in a truly beautiful manner.

Preferring custom development over installing off-the-shelf modules from the marketplace meant we could fulfil our client's specific requirements exactly, whilst ensuring the site remained incredibly fast.

Cutting-edge technology

The introduction of more bespoke elements such as Elasticsearch means customers can quickly find what they are looking for.

Instant auto-complete, search suggestions and the inclusion of CMS Content and Categories in search results makes Susie Watson Designs stand out above the rest.

With Elasticsearch customers can quickly search Products, Categories and CMS Content.

Data Migration

Bespoke Category Landing Pages showcase the Susie Watson Designs catalogue.

Migrating the Product, Category, Customer and Order data was an essential element of this project.

This is always a complex area, and you need experts at the helm for a smooth transition.

We worked closely with the Susie Watson Designs merchandising team to carefully map and sanitise the attributes and options to ensure a clean, stable data set in the new website.

We essentially became a part of the Susie Watson Designs team for the duration of the project, and remain so to this day.

Another Happy Client!

"Cheers!" as the site launches with our Guinea Fowl Straight Mugs!

The project was delivered on time and within budget including discovery, functional specification, development, data migration, project management and testing.

The website launch itself had a very tight window between product catalogue launches, and the team at Susie Watson Designs could not be happier.
