Magento Maintenance Service

Let us keep your sites secure and up to date with new features by installing the latest patches and releases from Magento

What we offer

Running a successful ecommerce site is hard work enough without constantly needing to keep track of the latest updates from your platform provider, along with the many nuances of web security in general. What we offer is a way to offload the planning and implementation of platform upgrades so that you can just concentrate on running your business.

By tracking the latest information from Magento we are able to provide you with an upgrade strategy to ensure that you are informed of the latest developments and always ready to act on them with our help.

For urgent security and bug patches we provide you rapid response times to apply the fixes to keep you secure. Then for each scheduled new version of Magento we provide staging of the upgrade allowing you to try out the new features and test your site on the latest version.

You can take a look at the currently available Magento patches at the Magento Security Centre.

Why do you need this?

To ensure that your Magento sites are running at their the optimum performance and are protected against known vulnerabilities we suggest to always run the latest available version in production. Adobe has committed to making a new version release of their Magento Commerce platform once per year and patch releases once every quarter.

The yearly "minor point" release generally introduces new features that you will miss out on until you upgrade. Patch releases are primarily focused on delivering security and quality enhancements on a regular basis to help you keep your sites performing at their peak. Without these updates your sites may prove to be outdated and insecure as the Magento software evolves.

With Absolute Commerce handling your upgrade strategy you can be assured that you won't miss a vital security patch and will always be ahead of the curve with the latest features.
